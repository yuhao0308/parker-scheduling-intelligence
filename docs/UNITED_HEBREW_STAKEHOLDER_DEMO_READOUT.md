# United Hebrew Scheduling Intelligence
## Current Demo Summary

**Prepared for:** United Hebrew and Z Semantics stakeholders  
**Date:** April 15, 2026

## Overview

United Hebrew Scheduling Intelligence is designed to help scheduling teams make faster, more consistent staffing decisions while continuing to use their existing scheduling system.

The current demo focuses on the highest-priority need identified in recent discussions: **same-day replacement support when a staff member calls out**. It also introduces an early monthly scheduling capability to show how the solution can expand over time.

## What the Current Demo Demonstrates

- **Same-day call-out support:** the system identifies an open shift, returns ranked replacement options, explains why each person is recommended, and records the final decision.
- **Overtime-aware decision making:** recommendations favor options that help reduce avoidable overtime pressure.
- **Better fit by unit:** the system prefers staff who are more appropriate for the unit and avoids poor-fit moves where possible.
- **Clear management visibility:** the demo provides visibility into recent call-outs, replacement decisions, staff workload, and overtime pressure.
- **Early monthly scheduling support:** the demo can generate a monthly schedule view, show coverage levels, and highlight open gaps.

## Alignment With United Hebrew’s Priorities

- **Respond faster to day-of staffing changes:** demonstrated through the same-day replacement workflow.
- **Control overtime more effectively:** demonstrated through overtime-aware recommendations.
- **Improve consistency in staffing decisions:** demonstrated through structured rankings and clear explanations.
- **Keep human judgment in the process:** demonstrated by keeping the scheduler as the final decision-maker.
- **Work alongside the current scheduling platform:** demonstrated by positioning the solution as an added intelligence layer, not a system replacement.

## What This Means For United Hebrew

The current demo shows a practical path toward:

- faster response to same-day call-outs
- lower avoidable overtime
- less dependence on manual work and individual memory
- more transparent and consistent staffing decisions
- better visibility for managers and leadership

## Current Phase

This is a **Phase 1 demonstration of value**. It is intentionally focused on the most urgent operational use case first.

Items planned for later phases include:

- resident-level acuity or bedside matching
- deeper resident-specific assignment logic
- full production integration with the live scheduling platform
- automated staff outreach and notifications
- United Hebrew-specific refinement of the monthly scheduling model

## Assessment

The current demo demonstrates that United Hebrew can introduce a scheduling intelligence layer in a practical, business-relevant way.

Most importantly, it already shows the core use case discussed as the top priority: helping schedulers respond to same-day call-outs with faster, clearer, and more defensible replacement recommendations. The early monthly scheduling capability provides a solid foundation for the next phase of development.
